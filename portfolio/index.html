Services

Services#

As a Data Science Consultant with over 5 years of experience, I offer a range of services that cater to businesses and organizations looking to leverage data for actionable insights, optimize processes, and drive growth. Below are the services I specialize in:

1. Machine Learning & Predictive Analytics#

Use data to make future predictions and automate decision-making processes:

  • Design and deployment of machine learning models (e.g., Random Forest, XGBoost, Neural Networks).
  • Predictive maintenance, demand forecasting, and customer behavior modeling.
  • Model development for credit scoring, market analysis, and trend prediction using Python, R, and SQL.

2. Business Intelligence & Data Visualization#

Transform your raw data into actionable insights with visually intuitive and interactive dashboards:

  • Development of dynamic dashboards using Power BI, Tableau, and AWS QuickSight.
  • Visual storytelling of data for business performance, sales, marketing, and customer insights.
  • Design and implementation of customized BI solutions tailored to specific business needs.

3. Custom Machine Learning Solutions for Businesses#

Tailored machine learning solutions to address unique business challenges:

  • Development of end-to-end solutions for credit scoring, customer segmentation, and inventory optimization.
  • Custom algorithms for specific industry needs, including real estate, finance, and e-commerce.
  • Continuous support and model maintenance to ensure scalability and performance.

4. Geospatial Analysis & Mapping Solutions#

Leverage geospatial data to gain deeper insights into location-based phenomena:

  • Geospatial data processing using ArcGIS, QGIS, and GeoPandas.
  • Map visualizations and topological analysis to support decision-making in urban planning, logistics, and real estate.
  • Geospatial econometrics and integration of spatial data with machine learning models.

5. Survey Analytics & Market Research#

Transform survey and customer feedback data into meaningful business insights:

  • Survey design, analysis, and implementation using tools like R, Python, and SQL.
  • Development of advanced survey analysis tools to drive decision-making and policy formation.
  • Customer segmentation and feedback evaluation for product and marketing optimization.

6. Data Engineering & Cloud Solutions#

Efficiently manage and process large datasets through scalable cloud-based solutions:

  • Design and management of data pipelines using AWS, SageMaker, EMR, Glue, and EC2.
  • Cloud-based architecture for big data analysis, scalable computing, and distributed systems.
  • Expertise in building and deploying data engineering solutions on AWS and Azure platforms.

7. Advanced Statistical Analysis#

Utilize statistical methods to understand trends, correlations, and patterns in your data:

  • Statistical modeling, hypothesis testing, and regression analysis using Python, R, and Julia.
  • Time-series analysis for trend detection, forecasting, and anomaly detection.
  • Expertise in econometrics for policy analysis, market research, and financial risk assessment.
